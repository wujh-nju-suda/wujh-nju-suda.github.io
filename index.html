    Biography

    Jin-Hui Wu is a lecturer in the School of Computer Science and Technology at Soochow University. His major research focuses on machine learning and its theories, especially on theories of flexible transmitter networks and complex-valued neural networks.

    Experiences:
    • Sep 2025 - Present: Lecturer, School of Computer Science and Technology, Soochow University.
    • Sep 2019 - Sep 2025: Ph.D., School of Artificial Intelligence, Nanjing University. (without entrance examination)
      Supervisors: Prof. Yuan Jiang and Prof. Zhi-Hua Zhou.
    • Sep 2015 - Jun 2019: B.Sc., Kuang Yaming Honors School, Nanjing University.
      Supervisor: Prof. Wei Wang.
